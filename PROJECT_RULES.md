# Romance Agent Lab — Project Rules

## Goal
Build an agentic AI system that assists in drafting romance novels using structured planning, persistent memory, and constraint-aware generation.

---

## 1. Core Narrative Contract

- The central romantic pair is locked from chapter one.  
  **Type:** Hard constraint

- Endings may be happy or bittersweet, but must resolve the central arc.  
  **Type:** Hard constraint

- External plotlines may dominate sections of the story.  
  **Type:** Soft constraint

- Breakups, emotional harm, and conflict between leads are allowed.  
  **Type:** Soft constraint

- Cheating arcs are allowed.  
  **Type:** Soft constraint

---

## 2. POV and Structure Rules

- POV must remain truthful (no unreliable narrator distortions).  
  **Type:** Hard constraint

- POV transitions are flexible but must be clear per scene.  
  **Type:** Hard constraint

- Flashbacks are allowed.  
  **Type:** Soft constraint

- Timeline jumps are allowed but must remain consistent.  
  **Type:** Hard constraint

- Chapter length may vary, but target length is 2000–3000 words.  
  **Type:** Hard constraint

---

## 3. Character and Age Rules

- Lead characters must be 18+.  
  **Type:** Hard constraint

- POV characters must be 18+.  
  **Type:** Hard constraint

- Romantic and sexual participants must be 18+.  
  **Type:** Hard constraint

- Minor characters (e.g., children or siblings) may appear as non-romantic side characters.  
  **Type:** Hard constraint

- Minor characters may not be POV characters or involved in romantic or sexual content.  
  **Type:** Hard constraint

---

## 4. Safety and Ethical Guardrails

- Power-imbalance romances are allowed.  
  **Type:** Soft constraint

- Dark romance themes are allowed.  
  **Type:** Soft constraint

- Violence is allowed in moderation.  
  **Type:** Soft constraint

- Explicit sexual content is allowed.  
  **Type:** Soft constraint

- Taboo themes are evaluated case-by-case.  
  **Type:** Soft constraint

---

## 5. Style Constraints

### Immersion and Voice

- Prefer narrative immersion over exposition. Show, do not tell.  
  **Type:** Soft constraint

- Avoid polite or balanced tone when it undermines emotional truth.  
  **Type:** Soft constraint

- Allow risky, slightly awkward, or unconventional phrasing when it strengthens voice or character specificity.  
  **Type:** Soft constraint

### Language Hygiene

- No em dashes.  
  **Type:** Hard constraint

- Avoid excessive use of stock romance phrasing (e.g., "her heart raced", "the silence was deafening").  
  **Type:** Soft constraint

- Avoid obvious clichés unless deliberately subverted.  
  **Type:** Soft constraint

- Minimize filter words and nominalizations.  
  **Type:** Soft constraint

---

## 6. Tone and Genre

- Supported genres include romance, romantic drama, erotic fiction, and YA tone.  
  **Type:** Soft constraint

- “YA tone” refers to voice, pacing, and emotional immediacy, not underage protagonists.  
  **Type:** Clarification

---

## 7. Continuity and World Logic

- Timeline math must be exact; elapsed time must be tracked consistently.  
  **Type:** Hard constraint

- Travel time and logistics must be realistic within the story world.  
  **Type:** Hard constraint

- Symbolic objects, secrets, and promises must be tracked.  
  **Type:** Hard constraint

- All planted mysteries and promises must pay off by story end.  
  **Type:** Hard constraint
