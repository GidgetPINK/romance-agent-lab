Romance Agent Lab – Project Rules

Goal:
Build an agentic AI system that helps draft romance novels
using structured planning and memory.


# Romance Agent Lab — Constraint Specification

## 1. Core Narrative Contract

- The central romantic pair is locked from chapter one.  
  **Type:** Hard constraint

- Endings may be happy or bittersweet, but must resolve the central arc.  
  **Type:** Hard constraint

- External plotlines may dominate sections of the story.  
  **Type:** Soft constraint

- Breakups and emotional harm between leads are allowed.  
  **Type:** Soft constraint

- Cheating arcs are allowed.  
  **Type:** Soft constraint


## 2. POV and Structure Rules

- POV must remain truthful. No unreliable narrator distortions.  
  **Type:** Hard constraint

- POV transitions are flexible but must remain clear per scene.  
  **Type:** Hard constraint

- Flashbacks are allowed.  
  **Type:** Soft constraint

- Timeline jumps are allowed but must remain consistent.  
  **Type:** Hard constraint

- Chapter length may vary.  
  **Type:** Soft constraint


## 3. Character Integrity Rules

- Character growth must be gradual and motivated. Sudden personality reversals are forbidden.  
  **Type:** Hard constraint

- Characters may not act against established fear/desire without explanation.  
  **Type:** Hard constraint

- Hidden identities are allowed.  
  **Type:** Soft constraint

- New major characters may appear, but must be integrated logically.  
  **Type:** Soft constraint


## 4. Safety and Ethical Guardrails

- All characters must be 18+.  
  **Type:** Hard constraint

- Power-imbalance romances are allowed.  
  **Type:** Soft constraint

- Dark romance themes are allowed.  
  **Type:** Soft constraint

- Violence is allowed in moderation.  
  **Type:** Soft constraint

- Explicit sexual content is allowed.  
  **Type:** Soft constraint

- Taboo themes evaluated case-by-case.  
  **Type:** Soft constraint


## 5. Style Constraints

- No em dashes.  
  **Type:** Hard constraint

- Style, tone, profanity, and metaphor density are flexible.  
  **Type:** Soft constraint


## 6. Continuity and World Logic

- Timeline math must be exact. Days/hours tracked consistently.  
  **Type:** Hard constraint

- Travel time and logistics must be realistic.  
  **Type:** Hard constraint

- Emotional transitions should be shown when significant.  
  **Type:** Soft constraint

- Symbolic objects and promises must be tracked.  
  **Type:** Hard constraint

- All planted mysteries must pay off.  
  **Type:** Hard constraint
